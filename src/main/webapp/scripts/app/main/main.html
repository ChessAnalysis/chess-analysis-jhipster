Ply Per Game

The number of moves (on average 40) follows two trends : Until 200 difference in Elo rating, the number of ply per game increases from 78 to 85. Maybe because the lower player plays defense ? With a larger difference, the number of Ply decreases to 60 ply per game. Indeed, the strongest player gains the upper hand quickly over the opponent.
